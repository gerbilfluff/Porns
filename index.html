Double Date - link
finished, 2006
Series: Batman: the Animated Series
Content: NC-17 (Two-Face/original minor female characters: hetero sex, anal, oral, brief scat; fetishized medical impairments; strong language; physical violence; threatened firearms violence)
Summary: When your every move depends on a coin toss, enjoying a night on the town can be anything but easy.
Author's Notes: The question's been posed to me more than once, by now. Okay, so... the things you write these characters doing. Does that mean you're into that kind of stuff?
   As this fic proves, sometimes the answer's "Nah. Maybe a couple bits of a scenario. Mostly, not so much." Hell, nearly all the fics I've done for this series began as nothing more than brainstorming challenges, rooted in the same idea: What would this particular character find arousing?
   Answers don't always have to be obvious. Sure, the Clock King could have a carnal attraction to timepieces. Gotham City and its denizens aren't known for being subtle, after all. Dig a little deeper, however, and it seems Temple Fugate's obsession with clocks might only be the most efficient means to an end his efficiency-crazed brain could find. Above all, he prizes organization; in its basest sense, organization equals control. Control the time, and you control people's actions-- you control everything. For a mind like that, domineering tendencies would be a cozy match, don't you think?
   Same with Two-Face, here. I may not consider much in this fic to be all that sexy (well... at least, not the guy/girl concept, heh), but when the first plot-point nubbins started snaking out from its what WOULD he be into? origins, it didn't take long for me to realize Harvey Dent's dark side probably had tastes most people would feel dirty just thinking about. And really, as the personification of every ugly thought to cross an upper-class do-gooder's pent-up imagination, why wouldn't Big Bad Harv find raw, unapologetic filthiness to be a turn-on?
   Shifting gears, I'd like to take a moment to thank the writing team of the recently-deceased Batman Adventures comic book series. Bear with me; this is related. It has nothing to do with sex. It has everything to do with making magic from a cancelled cartoon show's canon remains. I had the good fortune to discover the paperback volumes of this unsung gem midway through writing Caution: Plays Well With Others, and when it comes to this team's talent for penning characters as both in-character and grippingly, genuinely fun to read, I can only hope I've grazed the surface of their level.
   While I realize it might not be the most orthodox of homages to offer a respected party, this fanfic is dedicated to the Batman Adventures crew. It's like a Choose Your Own Adventure novel from the Eighties. Only with hookers.
   ...It was either this story, the Riddler word-puzzles-lead-you-to-his-secret-gloryhole idea, or the one about Victor Fries's guilt-ridden battle with his own necrophilia, so count your blessings, guys.
